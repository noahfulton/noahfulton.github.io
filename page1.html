God of War 1 and Prequels

God of War Ascension

This game's plot deals with when Kratos first defied Ares. As punishment, Ares's allies, the Furies, imprison Kratos within their titan prison. Kratos immediately breaks out and sets off to escape the prison.

The Furies use memories and illusions to further trap Kratos as he makes his escape. Kratos conquers the illusions, kills the Furies and escapes the prison.

God of War 1

Kratos, under Athena's instruction, sets off to kill Ares. Kratos encounters many enemies along the way. Some of which are familiar to him. In the end Kratos kills Ares by the power of Pandora's box. Kratos attempts suicide when he is told that his actions are not forgotten. Kratos is returned to the top of the cliff and is given the title God of War.
